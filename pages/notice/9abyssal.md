After the Wind 
by Marjorie Pickthall(1883-1922)

After the wind in the wood, peace and the night. After the bond and the brood, flight. After the height and hush where the wild hawk swings, heart of the earth loving thrush, shaken with wings. 

After the bloom and the leaf, rain on the nest. After the hills and the far glories and gleams,  cloud, and the dawn of a star, and dreams.


林中风止，夜色归宁。

羁绊与哺育之尽，雏鸟远行。

高处野鹰孤悬，万籁俱寂，

依恋大地的画眉，在振翅中战栗。

花叶已逝，雨落巢窠。

远山之外，荣光与微芒渐隐，

一片流云，一颗晨星初度，入梦。
